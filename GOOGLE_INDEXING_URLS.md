## How to Submit in Google Search Console

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Select your property: `hydrogenro.com`
3. Click **"URL Inspection"** in the left menu
4. Paste each URL above one by one
5. Click **"Request Indexing"** button
6. Wait for "Indexing requested" confirmation
7. Repeat for all 5 URLs

## Also Submit Sitemap

1. In Google Search Console, go to **"Sitemaps"** in left menu
2. Enter: `https://hydrogenro.com/sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**

This will automatically index all pages listed in the sitemap.
